Learn German

Our German courses are available in Morocco and Germany. Since 2019, we have been offering general German courses online and offline in Morocco, in partnership with our local partner. These courses range from A1 to B1, following the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). Online courses are conducted via Zoom Meeting.

Upon arrival in Germany, students continue their language training with an additional six-month course covering levels B2.1 and B2.2 before beginning their professional training.

Language Competency Levels

The CEFR categorizes language learning into three main levels:

  • A - Elementary Use of Language
  • B - Independent Use of Language
  • C - Competent Use of Language

Each of these levels is further divided into sub-levels, detailing specific skills and knowledge.

Detailed Language Levels

Level A1

Can understand and use everyday expressions and basic phrases. Can introduce themselves and others, ask and answer simple questions about personal details.

Level A2

Can understand commonly used phrases related to personal and everyday topics. Can communicate in simple and direct exchanges on familiar matters.

Level B1

Can understand clear, standard speech on familiar topics, handle common travel situations, and write simple connected text.

Level B2

Can understand complex texts on both concrete and abstract topics, communicate fluently with native speakers, and express detailed viewpoints.
